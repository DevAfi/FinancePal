## 🎯 **Financial Health Scoring Algorithm**

Our sophisticated scoring system evaluates your financial health across multiple dimensions:

### **Scoring Components**
- **💰 Savings Rate** (40 points) - Income vs expenses ratio
- **📊 Expense Stability** (30 points) - Consistency in monthly spending
- **⚖️ Category Balance** (30 points) - Diversification of expenses

### **Health Metrics**
- **🟢 Excellent (80-100)** - Strong financial health
- **🟡 Good (60-79)** - Stable with room for improvement
- **🟠 Fair (40-59)** - Needs attention
- **🔴 Poor (0-39)** - Requires immediate action
